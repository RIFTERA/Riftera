Info

RIFTERA is a futuristic beast-hunting survival game. Battle endless waves of wild beasts across four biomes, gather rare gems, and grow your Rift Hunter's power with skills, quests, and daily rewards.

For support or feedback, reach out through our X account linked below the menu.

Whitepaper

Overview

This whitepaper outlines the design philosophy, progression systems, and long-term roadmap behind RIFTERA's Rift Hunter Protocol.

Progression

Gems earned in-run fuel permanent upgrades through the Hunter Core skill tree, while daily caches and hunt-log quests reward consistent play.

Roadmap

Future updates will expand the Beast Codex, introduce new biomes, and refine the balance of skills and enemy waves based on player feedback.
